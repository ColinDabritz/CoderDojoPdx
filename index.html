Coder Dojo Pdx

Coder Dojo Portland, OR is an instance of the world-wide Coder Dojo movement. It's a coder night for kids, where K12 youth, along with their parents can play, explore, and learn about coding and building software. Right now we're finding our feet and figuring out what is right for Portland. Free tickets are required due to space constraints.
