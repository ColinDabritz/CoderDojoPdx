Welcome

Coder Dojo Pdx in Portland, OR is an instance of the world-wide Coder Dojo movement. It's a coder night for kids, where K12 youth, along with parents and mentors can play, explore, and learn about coding and building software.

Hello there!

We're still building our Dojo up, but we encourage everyone to grab a laptop (and free tickets) and jump in!

Our meetings are weekly. See the Calagator listing for details and get your free tickets at Eventbrite.
